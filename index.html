Overview

DappRadar is the world's largest dapp store — a platform where 2M+ monthly users discover and track blockchain applications, NFTs, and DeFi protocols across 50+ chains. I joined as Lead Product Designer to help the platform grow from a data-heavy tool into something approachable for mainstream Web3 users.

The problem

Blockchain data is inherently complex. The existing UI prioritized information density over clarity, which worked for power users but created high drop-off for newcomers. Research showed 70% of new users left within the first session without completing a meaningful action.

"People wanted to understand their portfolio at a glance — not decode a spreadsheet."

Process

I ran discovery interviews with both power users and newcomers to map their mental models. The key insight: experienced users valued density, new users needed progressive disclosure. I prototyped three navigation architectures, tested with 18 participants, landed on a layered approach — simple surface, depth on demand.

Results

+34%Increase in session depth for new users after redesign
2M+Monthly active users on the redesigned platform
−28%Drop in first-session abandonment rate
50+Blockchain networks in unified UI

Overview

Yellow Network is a layer-3 decentralized trading protocol built on state channels — technically sophisticated infrastructure that allows near-instant, cross-chain trading without custodial risk. My role was to design the trading interface and onboarding flow.

The challenge

Decentralized exchanges suffer from a UX paradox: the more secure and transparent they are, the more cognitive load they impose. Wallet connections, gas fees, slippage tolerance — the vocabulary alone loses most users.

"The technology was genuinely better — but it felt worse to use than centralized alternatives. That gap was my target."

Approach

I benchmarked 12 DEX and CEX interfaces. Key finding: professional traders trust density, but density without hierarchy is noise. I designed a three-tier information architecture — price and portfolio front and center, order book and trades one step deeper, advanced settings tucked away but reachable.

Results

3×Faster task completion vs previous interface
+41%Onboarding completion rate after new wallet flow

Overview

Openware builds white-label cryptocurrency exchange infrastructure. I designed both the end-user trading interface and the admin panel used by exchange operators across 12+ client deployments.

The challenge

White-label means the same product serves radically different clients: a retail exchange in Southeast Asia, a regulated fintech in Europe, an OTC desk. Each had different branding, compliance requirements, and users — but one codebase.

Design system

I built a token-based design system in Figma with theme variables covering color, typography, radius, and spacing. A single swap of 12 tokens could transform the product from dark crypto to clean fintech — without touching component logic.

"One system, infinite skins. The constraint forced better decisions than freedom would have."

Results

12+Exchange clients launched using the design system
−60%Reduction in per-client design customization time
