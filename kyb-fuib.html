    Overview

    FUIB Bank needed to digitize their Know Your Business (KYB) verification process — a legally required compliance flow for onboarding corporate clients. The existing process was entirely manual, requiring bank staff to collect, review, and verify documents by hand across multiple internal systems.

    The problem

    Manual KYB processing was costing the bank an estimated $1.1M+ annually in operational overhead. Each corporate onboarding took an average of 8–14 business days, creating significant friction for business clients and limiting the bank's ability to scale its B2B portfolio.

    "Every day a business client waited for verification was a day they could walk to a competitor."

    Process

    I conducted stakeholder interviews with compliance officers, relationship managers, and business clients to map the full verification journey. The key insight: 60% of delays came from back-and-forth document requests, not actual review time. I designed an intelligent document checklist that surfaced requirements upfront and validated submissions in real time.
